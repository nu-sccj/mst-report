Research Question 1: Physical and Psychological Health Concerns

What are the short- and long-term physical and psychological health concerns for young people who experience commercial sexual exploitation?

Overall Health

About 22% of the sample reported poor physical health for more than a week in the past month, while 50% reported poor mental health during the same period. Common chronic conditions included frequent headaches (51%), insomnia (47%), and asthma (25%). STDs were reported by 10%, and 2% reported having HIV. Mental health concerns were high, with 61% experiencing depression and 57% reporting anxiety. Among those with an STD, 19% self-reported experiencing CSE (commercial sexual exploitation), compared to just 2.4% among those without an STD.
